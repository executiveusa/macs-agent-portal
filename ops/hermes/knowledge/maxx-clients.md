## Lead Qualification Criteria

### High-Priority Lead (Route to Maxx immediately)
- Monthly revenue $10K+
- Existing ad spend $2K+/month
- Clear pain point (no content, no CRM, outdated website)

### Standard Lead (Enter nurture sequence)
- Monthly revenue $2K–$10K
- Has some digital presence
- Interested but not urgent

### Not a Fit (Politely decline)
- Looking for one-time cheap work
- Unrealistic expectations (viral in a week with $100 budget)
- Industry not served by team expertise
